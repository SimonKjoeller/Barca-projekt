Club legends

Lionel Messi

Messi has had 778 appearances for Barcelona scoring astonishing 672 goals and having 288 assists. Also reffered to as GOAT.

Ronaldinho

Ronaldinho, the king of samba. Only had 207 appearances, but still managed to score 94 goals and make 70 assists.

Guardiola

Guardiola is one of the few with succes as both player and manager. Had 263 appearances and 6 goals as a player, and after he retired he managed to coach Barca for 4 years.
